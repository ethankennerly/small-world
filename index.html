Big Cell

Play with up to 20 people in this small world.  Download here:

Windows | Mac

- Eat or be eaten
- Press enter to play
- Press arrow keys to move
- Eat smaller cells
- Be eaten by bigger cells

Game jam in Unity2D C#. Theme "A Small World" for Ludum Dare 38, April 21-24, 2017.
